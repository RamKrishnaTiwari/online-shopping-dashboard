# online-shopping-dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing online shopping performance, tracking total sales and profit across various product categories, US states, and quarterly periods between 2011 and 2014.

📌 Overview

This project features an Online Shopping Dashboard built in Power BI. It provides a comprehensive, high-level overview of retail performance by evaluating both sales volume and profitability. Stakeholders can use this dashboard to identify top-performing product categories, pinpoint the most lucrative geographic regions, and understand seasonal revenue trends over a multi-year period.

📈 Visualizations & Insights

The dashboard includes several detailed visualizations to break down online shopping data:

•	Total Profit by Category (Bar Chart): Highlights the most and least profitable items, showing Copiers, Binders, and Accessories as the top profit drivers, while items like Fasteners and Supplies generate the least.
•	Total Sales by Category (Bar Chart): Displays total revenue generated per category. Interestingly, while Copiers lead in profit, Phones and Chairs generate the highest total sales volume (nearing 200K).
•	Total Profit & Sales by State (Horizontal Bar Charts): Analyzes geographic performance, revealing that California is the dominant market by a significant margin (0.83M in sales and 0.17M in profit), followed by Washington.
•	Total Profit & Sales by Quarter (Pie Charts): Breaks down performance by time of year, showing a clear upward trend toward the end of the year. Quarter 4 drives the highest sales (458.6K) and profit (90.74K), followed by Quarter 3.

🎛️ Interactive Filters (Slicers)

A dedicated control panel on the right side of the dashboard allows users to dynamically filter the data:
•	Date Range Slider: Enables users to filter metrics for a specific timeframe between 1/7/2011 and 12/31/2014.
•	Category Slicers: Quick-select buttons to drill down into specific product categories such as Accessories, Appliances, Art, Binders, Bookcases, Chairs, Copiers, Envelopes, and Fasteners.

🛠️ Tools Used

•	Power BI: For data visualization, dashboard design, and interactive filtering.

🚀 How to Use

1.	Clone this repository: git clone https://github.com/your-username/online-shopping-dashboard.git
